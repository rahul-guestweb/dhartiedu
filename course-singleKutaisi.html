About Course

SKutaisi Medical University, located in the heart of Georgia, is renowned as one of the best medical institutions for Indian students pursuing their MBBS degrees overseas. This iconic institution offers a blend of impeccable academic credentials, comprehensive courses, and a vibrant campus life that makes it an excellent choice for international students.

One of the unique benefits provided by this university is their special consideration for Indian students' cultural needs. Acknowledging the importance of familiar food to the students' overall well-being, Kutaisi Medical University caters Indian cuisine in its cafeterias, taking the utmost care to match the distinctive flavors and nutritional balance found in traditional Indian meals. This thoughtful gesture clearly signifies the universityâ€™s commitment towards creating a congenial environment for its Indian attendees.

Kutaisi Medical University offers an MBBS program that spans over a period of six years, including one year of internship. The course curriculum has been meticulously designed to cover a broad spectrum of medical science disciplines from general medicine to specialized areas including pediatrics, gynecology, psychiatry, and surgery among others. Each department boasts well-experienced faculty members committed towards delivering comprehensive instruction and training.

The admission requirements at Kutaisi Medical University are straightforward and student-friendly. Prospective candidates need to have completed their higher secondary education with minimum scores in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology. Additionally, they have to clear an entrance exam and participate in an interview process. Upon being selected, international students are assisted by the university's international office with visa processing.

In conclusion, Kutaisi Medical University stands out as an esteemed institution offering high-quality MBBS education tailored for Indian students. Its thoughtful offerings such as specialized cuisine alongside a rigorous academic program make it a top destination for aspiring medical professionals from India.
