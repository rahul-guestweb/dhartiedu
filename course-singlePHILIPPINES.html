About Course

Lady of Fatima University is ranked as the number one medical university in the Philippines, offering students the best MBBS educational programs. The university provides all the necessary facilities and a conducive learning environment for its students. The MBBS program at Lady of Fatima University is designed to equip students with the requisite knowledge and skills needed to become competent medical professionals.

The MBBS course at Lady of Fatima University spans a duration of 5 years and 8 months, with the last year being the internship year. Students are required to have a senior high school diploma to be eligible to apply for the program. The curriculum involves a wide range of subjects like Anatomy, Physiology, Biochemistry, Pathology, Pharmacology, Obstetrics and Gynecology, Pediatrics, Surgery, and many more.

The Lady of Fatima University MBBS program also exposes students to clinical practices, where they get to interact with real patients and learn from experienced medical practitioners. During the last year of the program, students are required to undergo a clinical internship in a hospital setting, where they will gain practical experience in dealing with various medical conditions.

The Lady of Fatima University MBBS program is well-structured, challenging, and rewarding. Students are provided with world-class education and training that will set them apart in their medical careers. With the best and modern facilities, experienced faculty, and a conducive learning environment, Lady of Fatima University is definitely the ideal destination for students who aspire to become medical professionals in the Philippines.
