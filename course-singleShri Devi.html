About Course

Shri Devi Educational Trust, based in the picturesque city of Mangalore, India, is an esteemed educational institution that has made profound contributions to the landscape of higher education. The institute is known for cultivating an environment of innovative learning and academic excellence, offering a plethora of undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) courses.

Among the diverse programs on offer are unique courses such as Physiotherapy at both the bachelor's (BPT) and master's level (MPT), an integral program that merges theoretical knowledge with hands-on experience to develop skilled physiotherapists ready to take on clinical challenges. The M.Sc Nursing program is another empowering course that prepares nursing professionals with advanced skills and expertise, ready to play pivotal roles in various healthcare settings.

In response to the exponential growth and advancements of technology in our daily lives, Shri Devi Educational Trust also offers courses in Aeronautical Engineering, Information Technology (IT), and Computer Engineering. These cutting-edge programs integrate rigorous coursework with practical experience to equip students with the requisite skills and knowledge needed in these fast-paced fields.

These educational programs are designed not just to impart academic knowledge but also to foster critical thinking abilities, visionary leadership skills, and ethical values amongst students. Course details are meticulously delineated while requirements are clearly stated upfront so that students can make a well-informed choice about their future path.

In summary, Shri Devi Educational Trust offers versatile educational programs at both undergraduate and postgraduate levels, each one thoughtfully crafted to match international standards, meet societal needs and enhance employment prospects for its graduates. A student enrolled at Shri Devi Educational Trust can look forward to an invigorating learning experience that fuels individual growth while laying the foundation for a successful career ahead.
