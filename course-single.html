About Course

Located in Aktober, West Kazakhstan Marat Ospanov State Medical University is a leading medical institute in Kazakhstan that offers an excellent MBBS program to students who are aspiring medical professionals. To be admitted to the MBBS program, there are certain requirements that students need to meet. These requirements include having completed 10+2 or equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as the core subjects and having a minimum aggregate of 50% in those subjects.

With a team of experienced and dedicated faculty members, West Kazakhstan Marat Ospanov State Medical University provides an interactive and competitive academic environment that enables its students to excel in their studies. The MBBS program is designed to provide a comprehensive understanding of the human body, its functions, and various medical procedures and treatments.

The program also includes practical training and internships in affiliated hospitals, clinics, and laboratories. This enables students to gain hands-on experience in a real-world medical environment and prepares them for their future careers. After completing the MBBS program at West Kazakhstan Marat Ospanov State Medical University, graduates are equipped with the knowledge, skills, and expertise necessary to pursue careers in various fields of medicine.
