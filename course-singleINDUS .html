BSc (H.ons) in Aircraft Maintenance degree at Indus University focuses on practical skills that are used daily in the aviation industry. The students gain hands-on experience by working on real aircraft and simulators, supervised by highly experienced faculty members who have extensive industry experience.

About Course

Indus University in Ahmedabad holds a record achievement as the only institute in India that boasts a complete Boeing 737-200 Aircraft within its campus. An educational beacon of aviation studies, it offers an in-depth, rewarding Bsc.(Hons) program in Aircraft Maintenance.

The innovative curriculum incorporates hands-on training with the grounded aircraft, promoting not just theoretical understanding but also practical skills, indispensable in the real world of aviation mechanics. With the course integrated within an actual aircraft's structure, students are ensured to grasp extensive knowledge about aircraft operations, servicing and maintenance.

This meticulously structured program is divided into eight semesters spread over four years. Over the duration of the program, students will cover vital subjects starting from fundamental principles of aerodynamics to complex aspects of aircraft systems and technology. In particular, topics also include Aircraft Structures, Aerospace Propulsion, Avionics, Aerodynamics, and more.

The state-of-the-art labs and workshops further enhance learning experiences by simulating real-life scenarios and addressing advanced contemporary issues existing in the global aviation sector.

Admission Requirements for Bsc.(Hons) in Aircraft Maintenance at Indus University are as follows:

- Candidates should have passed their 10+2 examination with Physics and Mathematics as compulsory subjects along with one of the Chemistry/Biotechnology/Biology/Technical Vocational subject. - Candidates must secure at least 45% marks (40% in case of candidates belonging to reserved category) in the above subjects taken together. - Good scores in entrance exams like JEE Main or GUJCET can provide an added advantage during admission.

In conclusion, Indus University is committed to molding future aviation experts who are ready to take on professional roles successfully with their enriched expertise gained through this specialized program.
