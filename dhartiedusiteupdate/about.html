ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

We believe in providing the best educational & Professional choices for our candidates since 2001. Dharti International, Dharti education a part of its sister company dhartigroups started providing its service in domestic education in the year 2001 in Karnataka, with passion to bring international education closer to students who wanted to study abroad we started International education services in the year 2008. After grand success of Dharti International in Karnataka. Dharti education was introduced at heart of city Mehsana at Gujarath, where later on Mr.Satish Patel extended the opportunity of growing Dharti International to multiple cities like Ahmedabad and Mangalore(Karnataka).

What we offer?

Dharti International offers Study domestic Indian education or study abroad. we also provide Work at Abroad opportunities for well versed candidates offering a great career and life changing opportunities. Dharti Internatinal follows and abides by international state Rules & Regulations to ensure safety & excellent future for their candidates ensuring a study visa.
