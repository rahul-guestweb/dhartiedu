ABOUT OUR JOURNEY

Welcome to Dharti International, India's leading educational consultancy specializing in seamless international and national education opportunities. Our passion for excellence in abroad education has made us the most trusted partner for thousands of students pursuing a diverse range of Bachelors, Masters, MBA and MBBS programs across the globe.

At Dharti International, we understand that choosing the right path for higher education in abroad can be an overwhelming decision to make. Our experienced team of international and national education consultancy professionals is committed to helping you navigate through this challenging process. With extensive knowledge in various fields such as Bachelors, Masters, MBA, MBBS, and more, our dedicated team is here to provide you with unparalleled guidance every step of the way.

Our rich history as an educational consultancy in India stems from a deep-rooted belief in empowering ambitious individuals to attain success through global exposure and quality education. Be it pursuing Masters in Abroad or researching esteemed institutions for MBA programs, Dharti International has always been at the forefront in delivering exceptional results.

What we offer?

At Dharti International, our comprehensive range of services caters to every aspect of abroad education planning. Our expert team works closely with students to provide customized solutions that meet their unique goals and aspirations. Our suite of services includes but is not limited to:

1. Masters in Abroad: Pursuing a Masters degree overseas can unlock countless possibilities for a bright future. We guide students through every step of the process - from selecting the right program and university to assisting with admission applications and visa processes.

2. MBBS: We understand the dedication and hard work involved in pursuing an MBBS degree. Our team assists students in researching the best international universities offering MBBS courses while also ensuring a smooth application and enrollment process.

3. Bachelors: We assist students seeking a Bachelor's degree from world-renowned institutions. Our team closely works with aspiring undergraduates in finding the right fit based on their chosen field and career ambitions.

4. MBBS Programs: A globally recognized MBBS degree can open doors to successful careers across various industries. We guide aspiring business leaders through their graduate-level journey by recommending top-ranked international MBBS programs that suit their unique career goals.

5. National Education Consultancy: In addition to our expertise in international education, we also provide reliable guidance for students seeking opportunities in India's top educational institutions.

6. Visa Assistance: Our expert team helps to navigate visa procedures, ensuring a smooth process for students heading abroad.

7. Pre-departure Briefing: We understand the apprehensions and uncertainties faced by students before embarking on their overseas education journey. Our pre-departure briefings prepare them for a seamless transition to life in a new country.

Embarking on your journey towards Masters in Abroad has never been easier. Let our team of expert consultants provide the support and guidance you need to excel in the competitive realm of global education.
